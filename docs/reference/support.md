# Support

## HDR UK Technology Team

The HDR UK Technology Team provides support to Data Custodians throughout the onboarding process and beyond.

!!! hdruk "Contact us"
    - **Email:** [gateway@hdruk.ac.uk](mailto:gateway@hdruk.ac.uk)
    - **In-app support:** Use the **Need Support?** button on any page of the Gateway or Cohort Discovery site

---

## Getting started with onboarding

At the start of the onboarding process, an introductory meeting will be arranged with the HDR UK Technology Team to:

- Answer any questions about the service
- Define the exact tasks and responsibilities for your organisation
- Introduce you to key contacts within the team

The HDR UK Technology Team will not need to see your row-level data at any stage.

## BC|INSIGHT support

For the BC|INSIGHT commercial query tool, contact **BC Platforms** directly.

---

## OMOP mapping support

HDR UK and partners at the **Health Informatics Centre (HIC), University of Dundee** offer OMOP mapping services using CaRROT tools. Contact [gateway@hdruk.ac.uk](mailto:gateway@hdruk.ac.uk) for more information.
